Reflection
What I Learned

ClassPass is a fantastic company that makes me enthusiastic about my role and my work. In this project, I want to share three things that I learned:

  • 1 The localized design needs to think about both the local users' preferences and global scale-up.
  • 2 Visual design is a critical part of the overall experience. Oftentimes, keeping the design consistent is even more critical than chic.
  • 3 Business goals, team resources, and user experience are equally essential to success. Knowing how to prioritize these three pillars is a life-long course for a great product designer.
